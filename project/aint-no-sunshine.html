Next, the video deals with hierarchy, making the most important words larger.

Next, it deals with motion, using a system that reinforces the timing and hierarchy. The most important words simply appear, and the connecting words slide into frame on cue. Lastly, the atmosphere of the song is considered using a system of dynamic background color. The colors change according to the swells and dips of the song. The same colors are used repeatedly to give the viewer a sense of the song's form. An entirely different system is used for the bridge, which uses a different form and instrumentation, and therefore calls for a new, more commanding visual.
